Let's learn the basics.

Scroll down to learn the core conepts of stocks.

1) What is a stock?

A stock, also known as a share, is a unit of ownership of a corporation. Simply put, stocks are tiny pieces of a company you can buy or sell.

2) What is the stock market?

The stock market is where investors may buy or sell shares from thousands of different companies. These orders take place online, and share prices change daily.

3) How to make money in the stock market.

The way you can make money in the stock market is by buying and selling shares at different prices. For example, if you buy a stock at $5, it raises in value, and you sell it at $11, you make a total of $6 (before tax).

4) Good and bad habits.

An important rule to follow is to only invest with money you can afford to lose. Don't go buy stocks with next month's rent, there's no guarantee it will raise in value!
Something else to keep in mind is to never put all your eggs in one basket. Don't put all your money into one stock, you don't want to lose all of your money if one stock doesn't perform well.
